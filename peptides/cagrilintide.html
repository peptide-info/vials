Cagrilintide

Format: Lyophilized Powder (long-acting amylin analogue) · Vial 5 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

Long-acting amylin receptor agonist studied for appetite regulation and complementary metabolic effects alongside incretin agonists.

  • Amylin signaling contributes to satiety and delayed gastric emptying.
  • Often discussed in research stacks with GLP-1 / dual agonists (not as a casual DIY mix).
  • Weekly Sub-Q cadence in published titration schemes.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Nausea, decreased appetite, GI discomfort, injection-site reactions.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Do not aggressively stack with other strong GI-delaying agents without understanding additive nausea risk.
  • For laboratory research use only.

💬 Community notes

Anecdotal reports from forums and user communities — not clinical evidence. Experiences vary widely.
