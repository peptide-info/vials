Thymosin Alpha-1

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Ta1 / thymic peptide) · Vial 5 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

Thymic peptide studied for immune-modulation and T-cell related signaling.

  • Discussed in immune-support research contexts.
  • Often dosed a few times weekly rather than daily.
  • Distinct from TB-500 / thymosin beta-4.
