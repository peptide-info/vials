Thymalin

Format: Lyophilized Powder (thymic polypeptide bioregulator) · Vial 10 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A thymus-derived polypeptide bioregulator studied for normalizing immune function — supporting T-cell maturation and rebalancing cytokine profiles. Part of the Khavinson bioregulator research alongside Epitalon.

  • Investigated as an immunomodulator that helps normalize (not just stimulate) immune signaling.
  • Typically run as short courses of daily dosing, repeated periodically.
  • Distinct from Thymosin Alpha-1 though both are thymic peptides.
