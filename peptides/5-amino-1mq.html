5-Amino-1MQ

Format: Lyophilized Powder (NNMT inhibitor, small molecule) · Vial 50 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A small-molecule inhibitor of nicotinamide N-methyltransferase (NNMT) studied for metabolic and fat-loss research. By slowing NNMT activity in adipose tissue it is investigated for effects on cellular NAD+/energy metabolism.

  • Researched for adipocyte metabolism, energy expenditure, and body-composition themes.
  • Frequently studied as an oral compound; this sheet covers Sub-Q reconstitution like the other vials.
  • Effects are gradual — evaluated over multi-week research runs rather than acutely.
