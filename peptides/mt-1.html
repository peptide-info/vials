Melanotan-1 (MT-1)

Format: Lyophilized Powder (α-MSH analog, afamelanotide) · Vial 10 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A linear α-melanocyte-stimulating-hormone (α-MSH) analog that activates the MC1 receptor to increase eumelanin production. Compared with Melanotan-2 it is more selective for pigmentation and has little effect on libido or appetite.

  • MC1R-selective — tans with fewer of MT-2’s libido/appetite/nausea effects.
  • Shorter-acting than MT-2, so daily dosing is common during a loading phase.
  • Sun/UV exposure still drives the actual tanning response.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Facial flushing, mild nausea, and darkening of existing moles/freckles.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Have any changing or atypical moles checked before and during use.
  • Does not protect against UV damage — burning is still possible.
  • For laboratory research use only.
