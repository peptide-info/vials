Ipamorelin

Format: Lyophilized Powder (selective GH secretagogue / GHRP analogue) · Vial 5 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

Ghrelin-mimetic GH secretagogue with a relatively selective GH-release profile and lower cortisol/prolactin signal than older GHRPs in many discussions.

  • Pulsatile GH release when dosed away from food.
  • Often stacked with CJC-1295 No DAC / Mod GRF 1-29.
  • Common bedtime and/or morning empty-stomach dosing.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Head rush, flushing, water retention, tingling, increased hunger.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Avoid dosing near carbs/food if maximizing GH pulse is the goal.
  • Monitor for edema or carpal-tunnel-like symptoms with prolonged use.
  • For laboratory research use only.

💬 Community notes

Anecdotal reports from forums and user communities — not clinical evidence. Experiences vary widely.

  • Often used for: Sleep quality, recovery, and a “clean” GH pulse with less hunger/cortisol chatter than older GHRPs.
  • Watch for: Head rush, tingling, water retention, vivid dreams.
  • Avoid stacking with: Food/carbs in the peri-dose window if you care about pulse quality. Older GHRPs (GHRP-2/6) if you want the clean Ipa profile.
  • Safety vibe: Usually rated as one of the gentler GH secretagogues. Still a GH-axis agent — edema and IGF-1 sides matter at high frequency.
