🧬 Mechanism of Action

Short-acting GHRH analogue (often called Mod GRF 1-29) that amplifies GH pulses, especially when paired with a GHRP such as Ipamorelin.

  • No DAC → shorter half-life and multiple daily pulses rather than a flat elevation.
  • Classic stack partner with Ipamorelin.
  • Best away from food for pulse quality.
Not the With DAC version. If the label says “CJC-1295 with DAC” (or is unclear), use the With DAC sheet instead — schedule and sides are different.
