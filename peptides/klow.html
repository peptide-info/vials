KLOW

Format: Lyophilized 4-peptide blend (GHK-Cu + KPV + BPC-157 + TB-500) · Vial 80 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

Community “KLOW” blend = GLOW (GHK-Cu + BPC-157 + TB-500) plus KPV for an extra anti-inflammatory (NF-κB–related) research angle in one draw.

  • Typical vial: 80 mg total = 50 mg GHK-Cu + 10 mg KPV + 10 mg BPC-157 + 10 mg TB-500 (5:1:1:1).
  • Same repair/remodeling stack as GLOW; KPV is the differentiator for inflammation-focused protocols.
  • Dose rows use total blend mass. At 2 mL BAC, ~10 units ≈ 4 mg total → ~2.5 mg GHK-Cu + ~0.5 mg each of KPV, BPC, and TB.
