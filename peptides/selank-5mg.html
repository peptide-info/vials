Selank (5mg)

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Thymopoietin-like peptide analogue)

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A synthetic heptapeptide analogue of the naturally occurring immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin. It acts as a stable regulatory peptide in the central nervous system.

  • Modulates the expression of Interleukin-6 (IL-6) and influences monoamine neurotransmitter activity (serotonin, dopamine, and norepinephrine).
  • Exhibits pronounced anxiolytic (anti-anxiety) and nootropic properties without causing sedation or physical dependence.
  • Supports BDNF (Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor) upregulation, boosting stress resilience, adaptive memory, and cognitive stability under load.

🧪 Reconstitution Guide

  • Diluent: Add 2.0 mL of Bacteriostatic (BAC) Water gently down the side of the vial.
  • Mixing: Do not shake. Swirl gently until the solution is completely clear.
  • Final Concentration: 2,500 mcg (2.5mg) per 1 mL (equivalent to 25 mcg per 1 syringe unit).
  • Storage: Store in refrigerator at 36°F–46°F (2°C–8°C). Highly temperature-sensitive; keep chilled and out of direct light.

📅 Protocol & Dosing

Frequency: Subcutaneous injection administered 1 time daily. Typically run in acute cycles of 10–14 days, or used as needed for targeted stress periods.

Timing: Flexible timing, though ideally administered in the morning or prior to acute high-stress/cognitive demands.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Generally extremely clean profile. Rare instances of transient injection site irritation, mild fatigue if overdosed, or temporary changes in taste/smell sensitivity immediately post-administration.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Unlike traditional pharmaceutical benzodiazepines, Selank does not cause motor impairment, sedation, or withdrawal syndromes.
  • Discontinue immediately if systemic allergic symptoms or hives manifest.
