Selank (5mg)

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Thymopoietin-like peptide analogue)

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A synthetic heptapeptide analogue of the naturally occurring immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin. It acts as a stable regulatory peptide in the central nervous system.

  • Modulates the expression of Interleukin-6 (IL-6) and influences monoamine neurotransmitter activity (serotonin, dopamine, and norepinephrine).
  • Exhibits pronounced anxiolytic (anti-anxiety) and nootropic properties without causing sedation or physical dependence.
  • Supports BDNF (Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor) upregulation, boosting stress resilience, adaptive memory, and cognitive stability under load.

🧪 Reconstitution Guide

Recommended for nasal use: use sterile water (not BAC — benzyl alcohol irritates nasal mucosa). Final spray volume below matches a standard ~0.1 mL metered pump.

What you need

  • 5 mg Selank vial
  • Sterile water (not bacteriostatic / BAC water)
  • 10 mL metered nasal spray bottle (~0.1 mL per spray)
  • Sterile syringe + alcohol pads

Step-by-step (4 mL finished spray — common DIY method)

  1. Prep: Let the vial warm to room temperature. Wipe stoppers and the spray-bottle rim with alcohol.
  2. Reconstitute vial: Draw 2.0 mL sterile water. Inject slowly down the inner glass wall (avoid foaming). Swirl gently — do not shake. Concentration in vial: 2.5 mg/mL (2,500 mcg/mL).
  3. Build spray bottle: Add 2.0 mL sterile water to the empty spray bottle, then transfer the full 2.0 mL Selank solution into the bottle. Total volume = 4.0 mL.
  4. Final concentration: 5 mg ÷ 4 mL = 1.25 mg/mL → 125 mcg per 0.1 mL spray (~40 sprays before priming loss).
  5. Seal & label: Snap the pump on tightly. Label peptide, concentration (125 mcg/spray), and date.
  6. Prime: Spray into a tissue a few times until you get a consistent mist.
  7. Storage: Refrigerate the spray at 36°F–46°F (2°C–8°C). Keep unreconstituted powder cold/dark. Discard if cloudy, off-smelling, or after ~30 days.

Alternate denser mix: keep the full 2.0 mL vial solution in the spray bottle with no extra diluent → 250 mcg per spray. Larger bottles can be filled toward 10 mL total if you want a weaker mcg/spray — use the calculator’s Nasal mode with 4 mL or 10 mL volume.

📅 Protocol & Dosing

Frequency: Intranasal administration (nasal spray) 1 to 2 times daily. Typically run in acute cycles of 10–14 days, or used as needed for targeted stress periods.

Timing: Flexible timing, though ideally administered in the morning or prior to acute high-stress/cognitive demands.

Tier Target Dose Nasal Spray Application
Baseline / Standard Dose 250 mcg 2 sprays (1 per nostril) at 125 mcg/spray
Higher / Intensive Dose 500–750 mcg 4–6 sprays split across the day

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Generally extremely clean profile. Rare instances of transient nasal mucosal irritation, mild sneezing, a brief post-nasal drip taste, or temporary changes in taste/smell sensitivity immediately post-administration.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Unlike traditional pharmaceutical benzodiazepines, Selank does not cause motor impairment, sedation, or withdrawal syndromes.
  • Discontinue immediately if systemic allergic symptoms, severe nasal burning, or hives manifest.
  • For laboratory research use only.
