GHK-Cu (100mg) + BPC-157 (10mg)

Format: Split kit — 1× GHK-Cu 100mg + 2× BPC-157 5mg vials (10mg BPC total)

End state:

  • Blend vial (3 mL): 50 mg GHK-Cu + 10 mg BPC-157 → Sub-Q injections
  • Solo GHK vial (1 mL): 50 mg GHK-Cu → topical hyaluronic acid serum

🧬 Mechanism of Action

  • GHK-Cu: Copper tripeptide supporting collagen remodeling, antioxidant defense, and tissue repair signaling.
  • BPC-157: Healing peptide that supports angiogenesis, tendon/ligament repair, and local anti-inflammatory signaling.

🧪 Step-by-Step Reconstitution

Supplies: BAC water, alcohol pads, sterile syringes, 1 sterile empty vial, unscented HA serum / dropper bottle.

  1. Prep: Warm vials to room temp. Alcohol-wipe every stopper and the empty vial rim.
  2. Reconstitute GHK-Cu (100mg): Add 2.0 mL BAC water slowly down the glass wall. Swirl gently until dissolved (blue tint is normal). You now have 100 mg in 2.0 mL.
  3. Split the GHK into two vials: Draw 1.0 mL (50 mg GHK-Cu) into a sterile empty vial. Leave the other 1.0 mL (50 mg GHK-Cu) in the original vial.
    • Vial A (original): 50 mg GHK-Cu in 1.0 mL
    • Vial B (new): 50 mg GHK-Cu in 1.0 mL
  4. Reconstitute each BPC-157 (5mg) vial: Add 1.0 mL BAC water to each BPC vial. Swirl gently — do not shake.
  5. Build the blend vial: Draw both BPC vials (2 × 1.0 mL) and inject them into one of the GHK halves (either Vial A or B). That vial becomes:
    • 1.0 mL GHK + 2.0 mL BPC = 3.0 mL total
    • 50 mg GHK-Cu + 10 mg BPC-157
  6. Leave the other GHK half alone (1.0 mL / 50 mg) for the serum step below.
  7. Label both vials with contents, concentrations, and date. Refrigerate.
Final inventory
• Blend vial: 3.0 mL → 50 mg GHK-Cu + 10 mg BPC-157
• Solo GHK vial: 1.0 mL → 50 mg GHK-Cu (serum)

🧴 Solo GHK Vial — Hyaluronic Acid Serum

Use the remaining 1.0 mL / 50 mg GHK-Cu for topical serum (not for injection once mixed with HA).

  1. Base: Plain, unscented hyaluronic acid serum (no acids/retinoids in the same bottle), or an empty sterile dropper bottle.
  2. Sanitize: Wipe the dropper opening and cap threads with alcohol.
  3. Combine: Add the full 1.0 mL GHK solution into ~5–15 mL HA serum. Cap and invert gently — do not foam.
  4. Label: “GHK-Cu topical serum,” mix date, not for injection.
  5. Use: 2–4 drops on clean skin 1–2× daily. Patch-test first; keep out of eyes.
  6. Storage: Refrigerate. Use within ~2–4 weeks. Discard if cloudy, odd-smelling, or color changes beyond normal copper-blue.
Keep the HA formula simple — don’t park vitamin C or strong AHAs/BHAs in the same bottle with the peptide mix.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Injectable blend: Site redness, temporary blue tint, mild flushing.
  • Topical serum: Tingling or irritation — stop if a rash develops.
