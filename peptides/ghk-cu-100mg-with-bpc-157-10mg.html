GHK-Cu (100mg) + BPC-157 (10mg)

Format: Split-use blend kit — 1× GHK-Cu 100mg vial + 2× BPC-157 5mg vials (10mg BPC total)

Goal: Combine all three vials into one master mix, then split 50/50: half into a 3 mL injectable vial, half into a hyaluronic acid topical serum.

🧬 Mechanism of Action

  • GHK-Cu: Copper tripeptide supporting collagen remodeling, antioxidant defense, and tissue repair signaling.
  • BPC-157: Gastric-derived healing peptide that supports angiogenesis, tendon/ligament repair, and local anti-inflammatory signaling.
  • Together they are commonly used for systemic repair (injectable half) plus targeted skin/scar support (topical half).

🧪 Master Mix — Reconstitute & Combine

Supplies: BAC water, alcohol pads, sterile syringes/needles, 1 sterile empty 3 mL vial, unscented hyaluronic acid (HA) serum / empty dropper bottle.

  1. Prep: Warm vials to room temp. Wipe every rubber stopper and the empty 3 mL vial rim with alcohol.
  2. Reconstitute GHK-Cu (100mg): Add 3.0 mL BAC water slowly down the glass wall. Swirl gently until dissolved (blue tint is normal).
  3. Reconstitute each BPC-157 (5mg) vial: Add 1.5 mL BAC water to each BPC vial (2 × 1.5 mL = 3.0 mL BPC solution total). Swirl gently — do not shake.
  4. Combine into master blend: Draw both BPC vials completely and inject into the GHK-Cu vial (or transfer all three into one sterile container). You should now have ~6.0 mL containing:
    • 100 mg GHK-Cu
    • 10 mg BPC-157
  5. Mix once more: Gentle swirl only until uniform.
Master concentration (6 mL total):
GHK-Cu ≈ 16.7 mg/mL · BPC-157 ≈ 1.67 mg/mL

💉 Half A — 3 mL Injectable Vial

  1. Using a sterile syringe, draw exactly 3.0 mL of the master blend.
  2. Inject into a sterile empty 3 mL vial. Label: GHK-Cu + BPC injectable, date, and concentrations below.
  3. Refrigerate at 36°F–46°F (2°C–8°C). Protect from light.
Injectable half (3 mL) contains: ~50 mg GHK-Cu + ~5 mg BPC-157
Per 0.1 mL (10 units): ~1.67 mg GHK-Cu + ~167 mcg BPC-157
Per 0.01 mL (1 unit): ~167 mcg GHK-Cu + ~17 mcg BPC-157

Suggested Sub-Q dosing (from the 3 mL vial)

Frequency: Subcutaneous once daily (or split AM/PM if preferred). Site can be systemic abdomen fat or nearer a local repair target for BPC emphasis.

🧴 Half B — Hyaluronic Acid Topical Serum

Use the remaining ~3.0 mL master blend for a leave-on facial / scar serum. HA holds water and helps the peptides sit on skin more evenly.

  1. Choose a base: Plain, unscented hyaluronic acid serum (no acids, retinoids, or strong actives in the same bottle). A 15–30 mL dropper bottle works well. Or start with an empty sterile dropper and build a small batch.
  2. Sanitize: Wipe the dropper bottle opening and cap threads with alcohol. Work clean — this mix has no strong preservative beyond what’s in BAC / your HA product.
  3. Combine:
    • Simple strong serum: Add the full remaining 3.0 mL peptide solution into ~7–12 mL HA serum (roughly a 1:2 to 1:4 peptide:HA dilution). Cap and invert gently 10–15 times — do not shake into foam.
    • Small travel batch: Mix 1.5 mL peptide solution + 1.5–3.0 mL HA serum in a tiny sterile dropper; keep the rest of the peptide half refrigerated and blend fresh later.
  4. Label: Peptide names, mix date, and “topical only — not for injection.”
  5. Use: 2–4 drops on clean skin once or twice daily (face, neck, or scar/injury area). Allow to absorb before other products. Patch-test first.
  6. Storage: Refrigerate the serum. Use within ~2–4 weeks. Discard if cloudy, separated oddly, smells off, or color changes beyond the normal copper-blue tint.
Why HA? Hyaluronic acid is a hydrating, peptide-friendly vehicle for topical copper peptides. Keep the formula simple — avoid mixing into vitamin C, strong AHAs/BHAs, or leave-on acids in the same bottle (pH / stability issues).

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Injectable: Site redness, temporary blue tint (GHK-Cu), mild dizziness or flushing.
  • Topical: Tingling, dryness, or irritation — stop if a rash develops. Keep away from eyes.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Never inject the HA serum half — topical vehicle only.
  • Do not use BAC-containing mixes in the nose or eyes.
