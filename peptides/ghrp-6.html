GHRP-6

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Growth Hormone Releasing Peptide-6) · Vial 5 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A ghrelin-mimetic GHS-R agonist that triggers a growth-hormone pulse and strongly stimulates appetite — the most hunger-inducing of the common GHRPs.

  • Produces a GH pulse similar to GHRP-2 but with more pronounced appetite stimulation.
  • Frequently stacked with a GHRH (CJC-1295) for a fuller pulse.
  • Appetite spike can help bulking research or hinder a cut.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Strong hunger within ~30 min, flushing, water retention, tingling.
  • Can raise cortisol and prolactin at higher doses.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Avoid with active malignancy.
  • Marked appetite increase may be undesirable during fat-loss phases.
  • For laboratory research use only.
