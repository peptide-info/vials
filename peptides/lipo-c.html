Lipo-C

Format: Premixed Injectable Solution (MIC + L-carnitine lipotropic blend) · 10 mL vial

🧬 Overview

A ready-to-use lipotropic blend similar to Lipo-B but with L-carnitine added. L-carnitine helps shuttle fatty acids into mitochondria to be burned for energy, complementing the methionine/inositol/choline and B-vitamin components.

  • Lipotropic MIC blend plus L-carnitine for fat metabolism and energy support.
  • Premixed and ready to inject — no reconstitution.
  • One 10 mL vial commonly lasts ~2–3 months at typical weekly dosing.

📅 Usage & Handling

Guide

  • Ready to use: Premixed solution — draw directly; do not add BAC water.
  • Typical dosing: Commonly 1–2 injections per week (often 0.5–1 mL) Sub-Q or IM, alongside diet and exercise.
  • Storage: Refrigerate; protect from light. Discard if discolored or cloudy.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Injection-site stinging, flushing, or a fishy body odor from L-carnitine at higher intakes.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • A metabolism aid, not a standalone weight-loss treatment.
  • Avoid if allergic to any component.
  • For laboratory research use only.
