Pinealon

Format: Lyophilized Powder (EDR tripeptide bioregulator) · Vial 10 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A short Glu-Asp-Arg (EDR) peptide from the Khavinson bioregulator family. It is proposed to cross into the cell nucleus and interact directly with gene promoter regions, modulating antioxidant and neuroprotective genes rather than acting on a surface receptor.

  • Studied for neuroprotection, cognition, and antioxidant defense in aging models.
  • Typically run as short 10–20 day courses, repeated a few times a year.
  • Part of the peptide-bioregulator class alongside Epitalon.
