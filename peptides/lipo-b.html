Lipo-B

Format: Premixed Injectable Solution (MIC + B12 lipotropic blend) · 10 mL vial

🧬 Overview

A ready-to-use lipotropic ("fat-metabolizing") blend — typically methionine, inositol, choline (MIC) plus vitamin B12. These compounds support the liver’s processing of fat and provide an energy/metabolism nudge alongside diet and activity.

  • Lipotropic MIC + B12 blend marketed to support fat metabolism and energy.
  • Comes premixed — no reconstitution needed.
  • One 10 mL vial commonly lasts ~2–3 months at typical weekly dosing.

📅 Usage & Handling

Guide

  • Ready to use: The solution is premixed — draw directly from the vial; do not add BAC water.
  • Typical dosing: Commonly ~1 injection per week (often 0.5–1 mL) Sub-Q or IM, alongside diet and exercise.
  • Storage: Refrigerate; protect from light. Discard if discolored or cloudy.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Mild injection-site stinging, temporary flushing, or a metallic taste from the B vitamins.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • A metabolism aid, not a standalone weight-loss treatment — results depend on diet and activity.
  • Avoid if allergic to any component (e.g. B12/cobalt).
  • For laboratory research use only.
