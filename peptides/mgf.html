MGF

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Mechano Growth Factor, IGF-1Ec splice variant) · Vial 2 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A splice variant of IGF-1 (IGF-1Ec) released by muscle in response to mechanical overload. It is studied for activating muscle satellite (stem) cells and supporting local tissue repair after training-induced damage.

  • Investigated for satellite-cell activation and localized muscle repair.
  • Short-acting — often injected locally post-workout in research protocols.
  • The pegylated form (PEG-MGF) extends its very short half-life for systemic use.
