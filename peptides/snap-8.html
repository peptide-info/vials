SNAP-8

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Acetyl Octapeptide-3) · 10 mg · topical cosmetic

🧬 Overview

A cosmetic octapeptide (an extension of Argireline) that mimics the N-terminal end of the SNAP-25 protein. By competing for a spot in the SNARE complex it is designed to soften the muscle micro-contractions behind expression lines — a gentle, reversible, topical "Botox-like" concept.

  • Topical anti-wrinkle peptide aimed at expression lines (forehead, crow’s feet).
  • Reversible and far milder than injectable neurotoxins; effects are gradual.
  • Used in serums/creams, not injected — skin penetration is the limiting factor.

📅 Usage & Handling

Guide

  • Topical formulation: Dissolve in sterile/distilled water and blend into a serum base (commonly a ~3–10% peptide solution in the finished product).
  • Application: Apply to clean skin once or twice daily; results build over several weeks of consistent use.
  • Storage: Keep lyophilized powder cold; refrigerate the finished serum and use within its stability window.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Generally well tolerated topically; occasional mild irritation or redness.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Cosmetic topical use — not intended for injection.
  • Patch-test before regular use if you have sensitive skin.
  • For laboratory research use only.
