Melanotan II

Format: Lyophilized Powder (MT-2 / alpha-MSH analogue) · Vial 10 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

Non-selective melanocortin receptor agonist historically used in tanning and libido research contexts.

  • Stimulates melanogenesis (pigmentation) with UV exposure synergy.
  • Can affect appetite and sexual function via melanocortin pathways.
  • Nausea and flushing are very common on initiation.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Nausea, flushing, facial/lip darkening, freckling, spontaneous erections, yawning, fatigue.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Do not combine casually with PT-141 (overlapping melanocortin effects).
  • Monitor moles; seek dermatologic care for changing lesions.
  • Strong nausea risk — start low.
  • For laboratory research use only.
