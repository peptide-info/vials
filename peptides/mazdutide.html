Mazdutide

Format: Lyophilized Powder (GLP-1 / glucagon dual agonist) · Vial 5 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A once-weekly GLP-1 and glucagon receptor dual agonist built on the oxyntomodulin backbone. It pairs appetite suppression and glucose control with increased energy expenditure, and was the first glucagon/GLP-1 dual agonist approved for weight management (in China).

  • Dual GLP-1 + glucagon agonist for weight management and glycemic control.
  • Reaches maintenance with a short 2-step titration in trials.
  • Studied doses range from ~3 mg up to 9 mg once weekly.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Nausea, diarrhea, vomiting, decreased appetite; worst during dose increases.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Not FDA-approved (approved in China only).
  • Contraindicated with personal/family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN2.
  • For laboratory research use only.
