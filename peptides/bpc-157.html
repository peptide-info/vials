BPC-157

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Body Protection Compound-157) · Vial 5 mg

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A synthetic peptide sequence derived from a protective protein found in gastric juice, studied for angiogenic and tissue-healing signaling.

  • Supports growth-factor signaling involved in tendon, ligament, and muscle repair models.
  • Associated with fibroblast migration and collagen synthesis at injury sites in preclinical work.
  • Often discussed for systemic anti-inflammatory and mucosal support effects.

Targeting: Systemic belly-fat injections are common; some protocols prefer near a localized injury site.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Generally well tolerated. Mild injection-site redness, transient dizziness, or mild digestive changes.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Discontinue if unusual localized swelling or unmanageable joint irritation occurs.
