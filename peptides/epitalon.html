🧬 Mechanism of Action

Synthetic tetrapeptide studied in aging and pineal/telomerase-related research models (also called Epithalon / Epithalone).

  • Discussed for circadian / pineal signaling and cellular aging research themes.
  • Often run in short courses rather than indefinite daily use.
  • Sub-Q is the common research route on protocol sheets.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Mild injection-site irritation; otherwise often described as quiet.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Evidence base is mixed and largely non-US clinical; treat as research-only.
  • Do not assume “anti-aging” outcomes from marketing claims.
  • For laboratory research use only.

💬 Community notes

Anecdotal reports from forums and user communities — not clinical evidence. Experiences vary widely.

  • Often used for: Longevity / sleep-quality courses rather than daily forever use.
  • Watch for: Subtle effects; some report vivid dreams or nothing noticeable.
  • Avoid stacking with: Expecting a stimulant-like feel. Indefinite high-frequency use without breaks is uncommon advice.
