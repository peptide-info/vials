PT-141 (10mg)

Format: Lyophilized Powder (Bremelanotide)

🧬 Mechanism of Action

Synthetic peptide analogue of alpha-MSH. Non-selective melanocortin receptor agonist targeting **MC3R** and **MC4R** in the central nervous system.

  • Acts directly on the nervous system rather than the vascular system to modulate physiological responses.
  • Influences dopamine pathways to regulate libido, sexual desire, and erectile function.

🧪 Reconstitution Guide

  • Diluent: Add 1.0 mL of Bacteriostatic (BAC) Water gently down the side of the vial.
  • Mixing: Do not shake. Swirl gently until the solution is completely clear.
  • Final Concentration: 10mg per 1 mL (1mg per 10 units on a standard U-100 syringe).
  • Storage: Store in refrigerator at 36°F–46°F (2°C–8°C). Protected from light.

📅 Protocol & Dosing

Frequency: Subcutaneous injection administered PRN (as-needed) roughly 45 minutes to 6 hours prior to anticipated activity. Limit usage to a maximum of 8 doses per month. Do not dose more than once in 24 hours.

Tier Target Dose Syringe Volume (U-100)
Low / Test Dose 0.5 mg to 1.0 mg 5 to 10 units
Moderate / Standard 1.5 mg to 1.75 mg 15 to 17.5 units
High / Maximum 2.0 mg to 2.5 mg 20 to 25 units

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Transient nausea (often peaking 30–60 minutes post-injection), facial/upper body flushing, headache, mild nasal congestion, and transient injection site irritation.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Can cause a transient increase in blood pressure and decrease in heart rate after dosing.
  • Do not use concurrently with uncontrolled hypertension or cardiovascular disease.
  • For laboratory research use only.
